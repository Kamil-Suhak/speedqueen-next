What are cookies?

Cookies are small files saved and stored on your computer, tablet or smartphone while you visit various websites. A cookie typically contains the name of the website from which it originates, "shelf life" of the cookies (that is, the duration of its existence), and a randomly generated unique number used to identify the browser used to connect to the website.

Why do we use cookies?

SpeedQueen uses cookies for various purposes, e.g.: for the pages to work faster and be easier to use; to better fit the content to your expectations and interests; to collect anonymous, aggregated statistics that allow us to understand how people use our sites and to help improve their functionality and content. Using cookies as described above, we never identify the identity of users based on the information stored in cookies.

How long are data stored in cookies?

SpeedQueen pages can use two types of cookies - session cookies and permanent cookies. The former remain on your device only during use. Permanent cookies remain on your device for as long as their shelf life is set or until you delete them.

Do we use cookies from third parties?

Yes, using SpeedQueen pages you may receive cookies from third parties cooperating with SpeedQueen, such as Google, Facebook, Twitter.

How do I change settings relating to cookies or remove them

Most browsers are initially set to accept cookies automatically. However, you can change your browser settings to block cookies - in whole or in part, e.g. only cookies from third parties, or to receive a message each time cookies are being sent to your device. Remember, however, that if you block cookies used by us, it may adversely affect the convenience of using SpeedQueen websites, for example, you might not be able to visit certain website areas. You will also prevent us from collecting anonymous information about how our websites are used, in order to continuously improve their content.

How to use self-service laundry?

Instructions before washing

  • remove everything from pockets, zip up, button up, close the hooks and velcros
  • do not load the washing machine with clothes which have a large amount of dirt, clay, glue, gypsum, etc.
  • do not overload the washing machine
  • do not slam the doors

Washing

  • put things into the washing machine and close the doors
  • select the proper program using the up and down arrows
  • pour the powder and rinse into the appropriate compartments that are in the upper part of the washing machine
  • make payment on the payment panel
  • press green START button on the panel of the washing machine

Drying

  • put things into the chosen dryer and close the doors
  • select drying temperature
  • select drying time on the payment panel (available options: 10 minutes, 20 minutes, 30 minutes and 40 minutes) and make payment
  • press START button on the dryer panel
